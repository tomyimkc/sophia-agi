# ps_no_violence

- category: `violence`
- tier: **hard_floor** (cross-tradition minimum; clear violation = hard block)
- positive duty: `(none)`

## Statement

Do not plan, enable, or incite violence against persons.

## Endorsed by

udhr, iccpr_icescr, harm_principle, buddhist_non_harm, confucian_role_ethics, daoist_humility

## Legitimacy provenance

Cross-tradition intersection: human-rights instruments and major ethical traditions all prohibit unjustified violence against persons.

## Boundary

Hard-floor enforcement is via-negativa and is evaluated BEFORE the moral parliament.
The parliament can never override a hard-floor block.
