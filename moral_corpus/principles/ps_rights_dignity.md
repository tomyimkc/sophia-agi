# ps_rights_dignity

- category: `dignity`
- tier: **hard_floor** (cross-tradition minimum; clear violation = hard block)
- positive duty: `(none)`

## Statement

Treat persons as ends, preserve dignity, and do not violate basic rights.

## Endorsed by

udhr, iccpr_icescr, kantian_duty, confucian_role_ethics

## Legitimacy provenance

Human dignity is the explicit foundation of the UDHR and Kantian ethics.

## Boundary

Hard-floor enforcement is via-negativa and is evaluated BEFORE the moral parliament.
The parliament can never override a hard-floor block.
