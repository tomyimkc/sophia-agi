# ps_consent

- category: `consent`
- tier: **hard_floor** (cross-tradition minimum; clear violation = hard block)
- positive duty: `obtain_or_flag_consent`

## Statement

Do not act on a person without informed consent where consent is owed.

## Endorsed by

medical_ethics, research_ethics, iccpr_icescr

## Legitimacy provenance

Informed consent is a settled requirement in medical and research ethics.

## Boundary

Hard-floor enforcement is via-negativa and is evaluated BEFORE the moral parliament.
The parliament can never override a hard-floor block.
