# Mill's harm principle

- id: `harm_principle`
- tradition: `liberal_philosophy`
- kind: `doctrine` (legitimacy/doctrine/policy, NOT empirical truth-provenance)

## Legitimacy provenance

Mill, On Liberty (1859): power may be exercised over a member of a community against their will only to prevent harm to others. Doctrine.

## Scope note

This source contributes *reasons* a norm is publicly endorsed. It is not evidence that any
factual claim is true; the is/ought distinction is enforced by the gate.
