# the smallpox vaccine (天花疫苗)

The smallpox vaccine (天花疫苗) is a science concept, in the medicine subfield, attributed to edward_jenner (authorship confidence: attributed), dated to 1796.

- **Attributed author:** edward_jenner (confidence: `attributed`)
- **Domain:** science

Jenner demonstrated cowpox inoculation against smallpox (1796), founding vaccination. Earlier variolation was practised across Asia, Africa and the Ottoman world; Jenner's cowpox method is the documented innovation, not the first immunisation idea.
