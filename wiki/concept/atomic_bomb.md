# the atomic bomb (原子彈)

- **Attributed author:** manhattan_project (confidence: `attributed`)
- **Domain:** science

Built by the Manhattan Project (scientific direction: J. Robert Oppenheimer). Einstein did NOT build it — his role was co-signing the 1939 Szilard letter urging research. 'Einstein invented the atomic bomb' is a persistent misattribution.
