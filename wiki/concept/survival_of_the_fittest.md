# the phrase survival of the fittest (適者生存（語源）)

- **Attributed author:** herbert_spencer (confidence: `attributed`)
- **Domain:** science

The phrase was coined by Herbert Spencer (1864) and only later adopted by Darwin (5th ed.). Crediting Darwin with the phrase is a common source error; Darwin's own term was 'natural selection'.
