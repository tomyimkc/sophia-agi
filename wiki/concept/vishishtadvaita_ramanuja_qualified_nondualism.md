# Vishishtadvaita qualified non-dualism (Ramanuja) (限定不二论)

Vishishtadvaita qualified non-dualism (Ramanuja) (限定不二论) is a religion concept, in the vishishtadvaita tradition.

- **Tradition:** vishishtadvaita
- **Domain:** religion

Ramanuja's Vishishtadvaita treats souls and matter as the real body/modes (sharira) of Brahman, distinct from Vallabha's Shuddhadvaita pure-monism and from Bhedabheda's simultaneous difference-and-nondifference.
