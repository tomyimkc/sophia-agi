# LDS exaltation and the social Godhead of three beings

LDS exaltation and the social Godhead of three beings is a religion concept, in the latter_day_saints tradition.

- **Tradition:** latter_day_saints
- **Domain:** religion

The LDS (Mormon) teaching of exaltation toward godhood and a Godhead of three distinct beings is materially different from creedal Trinitarian Christianity's one-substance God, and differs again from the Community of Christ's more traditional theology.
