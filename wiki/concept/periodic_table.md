# the periodic table (元素週期表)

The periodic table (元素週期表) is a science concept, in the chemistry subfield, attributed to dmitri_mendeleev (authorship confidence: attributed), dated to 1869.

- **Attributed author:** dmitri_mendeleev (confidence: `attributed`)
- **Domain:** science

Mendeleev (1869) published the periodic system and predicted undiscovered elements. Lothar Meyer independently produced a similar table, and Newlands' earlier 'law of octaves' was a precursor; Mendeleev's predictive table is the consensus origin.
