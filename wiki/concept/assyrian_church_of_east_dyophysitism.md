# Church of the East dyophysite christology (two qnome)

Church of the East dyophysite christology (two qnome) is a religion concept, in the assyrian_church_of_the_east tradition.

- **Tradition:** assyrian_church_of_the_east
- **Domain:** religion

The East Syriac tradition confesses two natures and two qnome in Christ, a framework distinct from both Chalcedonian hypostatic union and Oriental Orthodox miaphysitism, and should not be flattened into 'Nestorianism' as a slur.
