# Eastern Orthodox theosis (deification) (东正教神化)

Eastern Orthodox theosis (deification) (东正教神化) is a religion concept, in the eastern_orthodox tradition.

- **Tradition:** eastern_orthodox
- **Domain:** religion

Theosis is participation in God's uncreated energies (Palamite essence/energies distinction), not the Roman Catholic created-grace model of sanctification nor the Lutheran forensic justification, so the traditions must stay separate.
