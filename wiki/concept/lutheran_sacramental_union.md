# Lutheran sacramental union (real presence in/with/under bread and wine) (圣餐合一)

Lutheran sacramental union (real presence in/with/under bread and wine) (圣餐合一) is a religion concept, in the lutheran tradition.

- **Tradition:** lutheran
- **Domain:** religion

Lutheran sacramental union holds Christ's body truly present 'in, with, and under' the elements without transubstantiation, distinct from the Reformed spiritual/pneumatic presence and from the Catholic conversion of substance.
