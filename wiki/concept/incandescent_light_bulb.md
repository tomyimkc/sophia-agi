# the incandescent light bulb (白熾燈泡)

The incandescent light bulb (白熾燈泡) is a science concept, in the engineering subfield, attributed to thomas_edison (authorship confidence: disputed), dated to 1879.

- **Attributed author:** thomas_edison (confidence: `disputed`)
- **Domain:** science

Edison produced the first commercially practical incandescent bulb (1879). Many predecessors (notably Joseph Swan, with whom Edison later merged interests, and Lewis Latimer's carbon filament) contributed; 'Edison invented the light bulb' over-credits a single inventor.
