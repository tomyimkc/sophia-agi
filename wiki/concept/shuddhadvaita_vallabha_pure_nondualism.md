# Shuddhadvaita pure non-dualism (Vallabha)

Shuddhadvaita pure non-dualism (Vallabha) is a religion concept, in the shuddhadvaita tradition.

- **Tradition:** shuddhadvaita
- **Domain:** religion

Vallabha's Shuddhadvaita holds the world is real and non-illusory while souls are identical-in-essence with Brahman without qualification, differing from Ramanuja's qualified scheme and from Chaitanya's inconceivable difference-nondifference.
