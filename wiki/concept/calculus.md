# calculus (微積分)

- **Attributed author:** newton_and_leibniz (confidence: `disputed`)
- **Domain:** science

Independently developed by Isaac Newton (fluxions, ~1665) and Gottfried Leibniz (published 1684, modern notation). Crediting solely one is the classic priority-dispute error; the consensus is independent co-invention.
