# Roman Catholic Filioque (procession of the Spirit from Father and Son) (和子说)

Roman Catholic Filioque (procession of the Spirit from Father and Son) (和子说) is a religion concept, in the roman_catholic tradition.

- **Tradition:** roman_catholic
- **Domain:** religion

The Latin addition that the Spirit proceeds 'and from the Son' is precisely what Eastern Orthodoxy rejects as uncanonical, so the two pneumatologies are distinct rather than interchangeable.
