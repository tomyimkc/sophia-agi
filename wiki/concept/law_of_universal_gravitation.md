# the law of universal gravitation (萬有引力定律)

The law of universal gravitation (萬有引力定律) is a science concept, in the physics subfield, attributed to isaac_newton (authorship confidence: attributed), dated to 1687.

- **Attributed author:** isaac_newton (confidence: `attributed`)

Newton stated universal gravitation in the Principia (1687). Robert Hooke had earlier suggested an inverse-square attraction, a priority Hooke disputed; the full quantitative law is credited to Newton.
