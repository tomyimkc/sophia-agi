# Oriental Orthodox miaphysitism (one united nature of Christ) (合性论)

Oriental Orthodox miaphysitism (one united nature of Christ) (合性论) is a religion concept, in the oriental_orthodox tradition.

- **Tradition:** oriental_orthodox
- **Domain:** religion

Miaphysitism affirms one incarnate nature 'out of two' and rejects the Chalcedonian two-natures formula, yet is equally opposed to Nestorian division, so it is a third distinct christology, not a variant of either.

> **Do not merge with:** chalcedonian_christianity, nestorianism.
