# Achintya Bheda Abheda (inconceivable difference and non-difference, Chaitanya)

Achintya Bheda Abheda (inconceivable difference and non-difference, Chaitanya) is a religion concept, in the achintya_bheda_abheda tradition.

- **Tradition:** achintya_bheda_abheda
- **Domain:** religion

The Gaudiya doctrine that the soul is simultaneously and inconceivably one with and different from Bhagavan is a distinct Vedantic position, not reducible to Ramanuja's or Vallabha's systems.
