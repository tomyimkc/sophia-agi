# Jodo Shinshu Other-Power (tariki) and shinjin (净土真宗他力)

Jodo Shinshu Other-Power (tariki) and shinjin (净土真宗他力) is a religion concept, in the jodo_shinshu tradition.

- **Tradition:** jodo_shinshu
- **Domain:** religion

Shinran's Jodo Shinshu makes salvation wholly Amida's Other-Power received as entrusting faith (shinjin), distinct from Honen's Jodo-shu emphasis on repeated nembutsu practice and from Tendai's self-power meditative path.
