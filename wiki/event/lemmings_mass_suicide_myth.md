# Lemmings deliberately commit mass suicide by jumping off cliffs (旅鼠集體跳崖自殺)

Lemmings deliberately commit mass suicide by jumping off cliffs (旅鼠集體跳崖自殺) is a history event, dated to mythologized_modern.

- **Domain:** history

Lemming populations fluctuate and they disperse en masse, sometimes drowning while crossing water, but they do not intentionally kill themselves; the belief was cemented by the 1958 Disney film 'White Wilderness', whose makers staged the scene by driving lemmings off a cliff.
