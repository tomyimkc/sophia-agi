# Bulls are enraged by the color red (公牛被紅色激怒)

Bulls are enraged by the color red (公牛被紅色激怒) is a history event, dated to mythologized_modern.

- **Domain:** history

Cattle are red-green colorblind; in bullfighting it is the movement of the cape (the muleta), not its red color, that provokes the bull, and the red hue traditionally serves to mask bloodstains.
