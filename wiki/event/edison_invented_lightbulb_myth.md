# Thomas Edison invented the first light bulb (愛迪生發明了第一個燈泡)

Thomas Edison invented the first light bulb (愛迪生發明了第一個燈泡) is a history event, dated to disputed.

- **Domain:** history

Incandescent lamps were demonstrated by inventors such as Humphry Davy, Warren de la Rue, and Joseph Swan before Edison; Edison's 1879 contribution was a practical, longer-lasting, commercially viable bulb and lighting system, not the original concept.
