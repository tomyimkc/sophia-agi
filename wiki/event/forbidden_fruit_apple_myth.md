# The forbidden fruit in the Genesis Garden of Eden was an apple (伊甸園的禁果是蘋果)

The forbidden fruit in the Genesis Garden of Eden was an apple (伊甸園的禁果是蘋果) is a history event, dated to mythologized_modern.

- **Domain:** history

The Book of Genesis never names the fruit; the apple association in the Western tradition likely arose from a Latin pun between 'malum' (evil) and 'malum' (apple) in the Vulgate and later art.
