# Albert Einstein failed mathematics in school (愛因斯坦數學不及格)

Albert Einstein failed mathematics in school (愛因斯坦數學不及格) is a history event, dated to mythologized_modern.

- **Domain:** history

Einstein excelled at mathematics from a young age, mastering calculus by about 15; the myth likely arose from a misreading of a Swiss grading scale (where 6, not 1, was the top mark) and he himself dismissed the claim.
