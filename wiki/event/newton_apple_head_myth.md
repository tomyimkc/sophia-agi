# An apple fell on Isaac Newton's head, instantly revealing gravity (蘋果砸中牛頓的頭)

An apple fell on Isaac Newton's head, instantly revealing gravity (蘋果砸中牛頓的頭) is a history event, dated to disputed.

- **Domain:** history

Newton's own account (via William Stukeley and John Conduitt) describes merely seeing an apple fall in his orchard, which prompted him to reflect on gravity over years of work; there is no contemporary evidence an apple struck his head or that insight was instantaneous.
