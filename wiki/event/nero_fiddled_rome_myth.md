# Emperor Nero fiddled while Rome burned (尼祿在羅馬大火時拉小提琴)

Emperor Nero fiddled while Rome burned (尼祿在羅馬大火時拉小提琴) is a history event, dated to mythologized_modern.

- **Domain:** history

The fiddle (a bowed instrument) did not exist in the 1st century CE; ancient sources disagree on Nero's conduct during the 64 CE fire, and Tacitus reports he was at Antium and organized relief efforts, though rumors of him singing later circulated.
