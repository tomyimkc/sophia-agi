# Columbus proved the Earth was round against believers in a flat Earth (哥倫布證明地球是圓的)

Columbus proved the Earth was round against believers in a flat Earth (哥倫布證明地球是圓的) is a history event, dated to mythologized_modern.

- **Domain:** history

Educated Europeans had known the Earth was spherical since antiquity (Eratosthenes even measured its circumference); the real dispute with Columbus was that he badly underestimated the Earth's size, and his critics were correct that Asia was too far to reach.
