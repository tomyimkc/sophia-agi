# Sealing of Magna Carta (1215) (大憲章封印（1215）)

Sealing of Magna Carta (1215) (大憲章封印（1215）) is a history event, dated to 1215-06-15.

- **Domain:** history

Dated charter with surviving 1215 exemplifications. Myth-trap: popularly mythologized as the origin of modern democracy and universal human rights — it was a feudal settlement between King John and barons, and most clauses were soon annulled or repealed.
