# Mrs. O'Leary's cow started the Great Chicago Fire by kicking over a lantern (歐萊里太太的牛引發芝加哥大火)

Mrs. O'Leary's cow started the Great Chicago Fire by kicking over a lantern (歐萊里太太的牛引發芝加哥大火) is a history event, dated to mythologized_modern.

- **Domain:** history

The fire of 1871 did begin near the O'Leary barn, but the cow-and-lantern story was invented by a reporter who later admitted fabricating it, and the Chicago City Council formally exonerated Catherine O'Leary in 1997.
