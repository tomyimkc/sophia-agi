title: a dead heartbeat means the loop itself blocked
---

The poller is non-blocking and commits a heartbeat EVERY tick while a job runs. So a dead heartbeat cannot be a long job — the tick loop must be blocked. Most likely a network call with no timeout. Suspect the git operations in _sync()/_push_with_retry().
