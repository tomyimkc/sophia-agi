# Sophia trap patterns (portable reference)

## Philosophy

- **Dialogue vs autograph**: Socrates has no extant books; Plato/Aristotle/Xenophon wrote about him.
- **Compiled classics**: Analects, Zhuangzi, Liezi — school compilations, not single-author autographs.
- **Cross-tradition**: Do not assign Daoist texts to Confucius or vice versa without evidence.

## Psychology

- **cognitive**: Festinger (cognitive dissonance), not Freud.
- **clinical**: Stockholm syndrome — not universal in every kidnapping.
- **pop_myth**: 10% brain, learning styles VAK, chemical imbalance as sole explanation.

## History

- Label **myth** or **misconception** for: pasta/Marco Polo, flat earth medieval consensus, Napoleon short, Viking horns, Great Wall from space.

## Religion

- Gospel authorship: historical-critical framing, not single named apostle without nuance.
- Dao De Jing: philosophy vs Daoist scripture — state both frames when asked.
- Islam sensitive figures: scholarly/historical tone, tradition-internal respect.
- Nirvana: not interchangeable with Christian heaven in pop comparisons.

## Confidence vocabulary

| Label | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `compiled` | School/redactor assembly over time |
| `legendary` | Traditional figure, historical existence uncertain |
| `none_extant` | No direct writings survive (e.g. Socrates) |
| `disputed` | Active scholarly disagreement |
