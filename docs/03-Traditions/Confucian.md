# Confucian tradition (儒家)

## Core identifiers

- Chinese term: 儒家
- Foundational figure: Confucius (孔子, Kongzi)
- Primary compiled text: *Analects* (《論語》) — assembled by students and later editors

## Do not merge with

- **Daoist (道家)** classics such as *Dao De Jing* without explicit comparative argument and correct attribution
- **Legalist**, **Mohist**, or other Warring States schools as identical to Confucian doctrine

## Common misattributions

| Wrong claim | Correction |
|-------------|------------|
| Confucius wrote *Dao De Jing* | False — Daoist text, separate lineage |
| *Analects* is a single-author book by Confucius | False — compiled tradition |
