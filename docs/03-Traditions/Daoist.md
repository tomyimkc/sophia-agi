# Daoist tradition (道家)

## Core identifiers

- Chinese term: 道家
- Foundational text (traditional): *Dao De Jing* (《道德經》)
- Traditional figure: Laozi (老子) — historicity disputed

## Do not merge with

- **Confucian (儒家)** teachings as if one school authored the other's classics

## Common misattributions

| Wrong claim | Correction |
|-------------|------------|
| Confucius authored *Dao De Jing* | False |
| Laozi certainly existed as a single historical author | Uncertain — treat as legendary or composite unless new evidence appears |
