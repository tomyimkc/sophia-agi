## Nice Classification — recommended classes

The Nice Classification (used by HK, US, and Madrid) groups goods/services. For an
AI-software research project the relevant classes are **9**, **42**, and optionally **41**.

### Class 9 — software (goods)
> Downloadable and recorded computer software for artificial intelligence; downloadable
> computer software using large language models for verifying factual claims against
> source documents and for detecting and preventing fabricated or misattributed content;
> downloadable software for provenance tracking, source attribution, and abstention in
> automated reasoning systems; computer software development kits (SDK); recorded data
> sets for training and evaluating artificial-intelligence models.

### Class 42 — software services (SaaS / development / research)
> Software as a service (SaaS) featuring artificial-intelligence software for verifying,
> attributing, and source-grounding machine-generated text; platform as a service (PaaS)
> for fact-checking, hallucination detection, and content provenance; design, development,
> and maintenance of computer software and artificial-intelligence models; scientific and
> technological research in the field of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and
> trustworthy/verifiable AI; providing online non-downloadable software for automated
> claim verification and abstention.

### Class 41 — education / research publishing (optional)
> Providing online educational materials, benchmarks, and research publications in the
> field of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and verifiable reasoning; arranging
> and conducting of research benchmarks and leaderboards.

## Before you file (clearance checklist)
- [ ] **Search existing marks** — HK IPD online search (https://esearch.ipd.gov.hk),
      USPTO TESS, and WIPO Global Brand Database — for each mark in classes 9/42/41.
      Pay special attention to **WISDOM GATE** and **MORAL GATE** (common phrases).
- [ ] Confirm the applicant name/address exactly matches your ID (HK filings list the
      applicant of record publicly).
- [ ] Decide HK-only vs. HK + USPTO vs. **Madrid System** (one application → multiple
      countries; HK is *not* yet a Madrid member for filings *originating* in HK, so a
      base US/other filing may be needed to use Madrid — confirm current status with an
      agent).
- [ ] Keep dated specimens of use (screenshots of the README, site, releases showing the
      marks in commerce) — useful for both registration and any later dispute.

## Indicative HK fees (verify current rates with IPD)
Official filing fee is on the order of a few thousand HKD for one class, with additional
fees per extra class; agent fees are separate. Treat this as the cheapest, most
enforceable IP step available to you.
